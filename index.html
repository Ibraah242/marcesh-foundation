A Message From Our Founder

"Every child deserves a chance to dream, to learn, and to grow in a nurturing environment. At Marcesh Charty Foundation, we're committed to turning these dreams into reality for orphans and underprivileged children across Tanzania."

Our journey began with a simple belief: that no child should be denied basic education, proper nutrition, or the warmth of a loving community. What started as small visits to local schools has grown into a movement that has touched thousands of young lives.

We believe that by investing in children today, we're building a better tomorrow for our entire nation. Each notebook, each uniform, each meal we provide is more than just material assistance—it's a message of hope, a promise that someone cares, and an invitation to dream bigger.

Mr. Juma Marcesh

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Social Work & Community Development (University of Dar es Salaam)

15+ Years Experience in Nonprofit Leadership
