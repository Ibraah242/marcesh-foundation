About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Mr. Marcesh Daniel, the Marcesh Charty Foundation was born out of a deep concern for the plight of orphans and underprivileged children in Tanzania. After witnessing the challenges these children face in accessing basic education and necessities, Mr. Daniel committed to making a difference.

What started as small-scale visits to orphanages and schools has grown into a registered non-profit organization that has impacted over 1,200 children across 35+ schools in Tanzania. Our work focuses on providing not just material support, but also emotional encouragement and hope for a better future.

Our Vision

A Tanzania where every child, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, basic necessities, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To improve the lives of orphans and underprivileged children by providing educational support, essential supplies, and emotional encouragement through school visits and community outreach programs.

Our Core Values
